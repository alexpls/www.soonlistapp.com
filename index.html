Soonlist for iPhone

Get reminders when your favourite movies are coming out!

  • Soonlist server to get the latest upcoming movie info and release dates
  • Create custom reminders for before (or even after) a movie you're interested in is coming out
  • iOS 9 features: 3D Touch? Check. Spotlight integration? Check.

Not on your iPhone? Not a worry. Email yourself a reminder to download Soonlist. (Your email address will be used for a one-time email and then will be discarded).
